Welcome to Boston Squash Club, Boston's premier squash facility. We provide lessons to individuals of all experience from beginner to advanced. Our mission is to build our community through the sport of squash as well as develop skill and technique from our amazing group of trainers. We provide private lessons, group clinics, and have open court sessions on a daily basis.
