About

History

Founded in 1960, Boston Squash Club has been the epicenter of squash in the greater Boston Area. Opening its first location in 1960 in Cambridge, BSC has hosted PSA professionals include Mohammed Elshorbagy, Ali Farag (Harvard University 2014), and Gregory Gaultier. Boston Squash Club hosts numerous junior and senior tournaments each year. Our board of trustees are committed to moving BSC as a global leader in squash development and the squash community.

About Our Facilities

Boston Squash Club has 30 singles courts and 6 doubles courts designed and manufactured by ABS across our three facilities. In our Cambridge location, we have 10 singles courts and 2 doubles courts. In our Medford location, there are 10 singles courts and 2 doubles court. Finally, in our Boston location, there are 10 singles courts and 2 doubles courts. In each location, we also have an endurance training facility with professional staff and equipment.

Programs

We have a range of clinics based on skill level. BSC also offers private lessons based on membership status. Please purchase a membership type prior to using our facilities. Additionally, refer to the schedule for event and clinic times.
